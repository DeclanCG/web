Conclusion

Within my Animation, I only had a short period of time to create this in (2 weeks roughly). But none the less I started off with my planning originaly having the idea of a lake creature attacking a fisherman but changing the idea to this. The whole idea behind "Portal" was heavily inspired by God of War 2018 as I had played the game and thought about the fast travel door system. With my planning I had looked into some norse mythology and the different realms, I had done some reasearch into the origin of old norse writing and found a template of their alphabet which I copied to make some words in norse. Further onto the animation I had cut out some of the assets I was going to use like the huge symbol on the island and the symbol on the door as time was short. But none the less I continued on the plan of making my character open up on the ocean traveling to the main island, traversing through the dark cave to finish up with braking the locks on the portal door and traveling to the next area. I had thought about the different colours to use as keeping the island a variety of grey's for the texture of rock and the sand a certain yellow colour. As for the character I tried sticking to the reference image and using the type of colours shown there, but for the boat, I had tried different versions of brown, lighter and darker to create a contrast on the character and the waterline.
